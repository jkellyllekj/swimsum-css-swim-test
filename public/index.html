Find your Critical Swim Speed

CSS estimates your threshold pace: the fastest speed you can hold for longer efforts without fading hard.

  1. 1Swim a hard 400m time trial.
  2. 2Rest fully, then swim a hard 200m time trial.
  3. 3Enter both times to get CSS pace and training guidance.

What the result means

The difference between your 200m and 400m performances helps classify your engine profile.

  • Diesel: good endurance, needs speed/power work.
  • Balanced: strong mix of speed and endurance.
  • Sprinter: fast speed, needs aerobic endurance support.

Benchmarks (pace per 100m)

World Class: 0:58-1:05 | Competitive: 1:10-1:25 | Intermediate: 1:30-1:50 | Novice: 2:00+
